What is Vsauce?

Vsauce is an educational youtube channel started by Michael Stevens on June 24, 2010. Vsauce has now expanded into an entire team and consits of three channels Vsauce 1, 2, and 3. Interestingly the name "Vsauce" originally came from a random name generator Michael was playing with.

Who is Vsauce

Vsauce conits of three hosts, Michael Stevens the founder, Kevin Lieber, and Jake Roper as well as a whole team working behind the scenes. The three of them run the channels Vsauce, Vsauce 2, and Vsauce 3 respectively. All three hosts have a slightly difrent style in their videos but they all produce entertaining educational videos targeted at all age groups.
