AVANT GARDE GOTHIC TYPE SPECIMEN

ABOUT THIS PROJECT

This poster was a homework assignment for my text class. The objective of the assignment was to make a poster showing off a particular typeface, what makes it quique, some of its history, and the typeface being used in various weights and settings.
