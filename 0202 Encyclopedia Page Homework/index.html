What is Vsauce?

Vsauce is an educational youtube channel started by Michael Stevens on June 24, 2010.

Who is Vsauce

Vsauce conits of three members, Michael Stevens the founder, Kevin Lieber, and Jake Roper. They run the channels Vsauce, Vsauce 2, and Vsauce 3 respectively. All three members have a slightly difrent style in their videos but they all produce entertaining educational videos targeted at all age groups.

Michael Stevens

Michael got the name "Vsauce" from a random name generator he found.
